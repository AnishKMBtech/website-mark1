Movies

Movies are my escape from reality. Whether it's a mind-bending sci-fi or a feel-good flick, I'm all in. Analyzing storytelling techniques? Sure, if by analyzing you mean quoting lines and pretending I'm the protagonist. I'm particularly fascinated by Edward Snowden's story, physics-related documentaries, and anything directed by David Fincher or starring Brad Pitt, Leonardo DiCaprio, Jake Gyllenhaal, Andrew Garfield, and Tobey Maguire. I love physics and enjoy movies featuring Tom Cruise and Stanley Marvel's creations.
